Cold Spring Park Art Installation

A temporary installation by artist Marty Cain, funded by the National Endowment for the Arts, was placed in various locations in Cold Spring Park during the summer of 1993. "Art in Nature," by Marty Cain, consisted of several installations near the soccer fields and along the exercise trail.
